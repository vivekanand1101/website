01. About

I grew up in Bihar, where summers smell of mango and monsoons turn everything impossibly green. That's still where home is.

I've been writing software professionally for 8+ years now, mostly backend systems, databases, and the kind of plumbing that keeps products running at scale. Fintech, KYC platforms, real-time systems serving millions of users. I'm a Google Summer of Code 2016 alumnus (Fedora/Pagure), and that was my first real taste of open source.

I think best in a terminal. My editor is Vim, my shell is zsh, and I genuinely enjoy reading man pages. I build with Python, Go, TypeScript, Django, PostgreSQL, and whatever else the problem demands.

Outside of work, I play cricket. Not the TV kind. The gully kind, with a tennis ball and arguments about LBW. I also built a scoreboard app for our local village tournament because of course I did.

03. The Journey

Bihar

Where It Started

Grew up in rural Bihar. First encounter with a computer was at a cybercafe. The blinking cursor on a black screen felt like magic. Still does.

IIIT Allahabad

College

Studied at the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad. Spent more time on IRC and open-source mailing lists than in classrooms. No regrets.

2016

Google Summer of Code, Fedora

Selected for GSoC with Fedora. Built tooling for Pagure, Fedora's git forge. Wrote patches, argued on IRC, shipped features. First real taste of open source at scale.

Ethereal Machines

First Job

Jumped into the startup world. Backend systems, getting my hands dirty with everything from databases to deployment pipelines.

PaySense

Fintech

Dove into fintech. Built systems around lending, KYC, and payments. Learned what it means to build software where getting it wrong costs real money.

Innovaccer

Healthcare Tech

Worked on healthcare data platforms at scale. Big data, complex integrations, and systems that actually matter to people's lives.

Barq

Fintech, Again

Back in fintech. Built a payments app from the ground up. Performance, security, scalability. Led the accounts team as Technical Lead.

Virtual Internships

Present

Tech Lead. Building the platform that connects students worldwide with remote internship experiences. Still shipping code, still in the terminal.
